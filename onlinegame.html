⚡ OCEANIC MATE DUEL – RULES
• You and opponent start with 6 random cards (face up).
• Click a card → it fights a random opponent card in the center.
• Win: you steal 1 random card from opponent's hand → add to your collection pot.
• Lose: opponent steals 1 random card from your hand → adds to their pot.
• Tie: both cards discarded, each draws 1 from deck (no steal).
• After each fight, click “Next round” to continue.
• First to collect 10 cards in your pot wins the duel!
