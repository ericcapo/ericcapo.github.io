Postdoctoral researchers

Corentin Fournier
Postdoctoral Researcher
Corentin Fournier is a postdoc in Capo lab co-supervised by Eric Libby (IceLab). His work aims to apply microbial metabolic models into environmental genomics data to better understand biogeochemical interactions in past and modern environments, with a primary focus on Baltic Sea and Swedish West Coast fjords.
📧 corentin.fournier@umu.se
Flúvio Modolon
Postdoctoral Researcher
Flúvio focus is a post-doc in David Wardle group investigating how soil microbial communities shape and respond to ecological processes across a chronosequence, i.e., changes in organic matter stocks, nutrient fluxes, decomposition, and experimentally induced loss of plant diversity.
📧 fluvio.modolon@umu.se
Isabel Sanz-Sáez
Postdoctoral Researcher
Isabel Sanz-Sáez is a postdoc in Erik Björn group at the Chemistry Department of Umeå University. Her research focuses on exploring the intricate relationships between microbes and environmental pollutants, including both legacy and emerging contaminants.
📧 isabel.sanz@umu.se
Dongna Yan
Postdoctoral Researcher
Dongna Yan is a postdoc working in Zhisheng An group at the Institute of Earth Environment (Chinese Academy of Sciences, Xi'an, Shaanxi). Her current research focuses on the study of past ecological environmental changes recorded by sedDNA and geochemical indicators.
📧 yandn@ieecas.cn
Li Zhao
Postdoctoral Researcher
Li Zhao is a post-doc working in the field of marine microbial ecology in Agneta Andersson group, focusing on how dissolved organic matter (DOM) influences microbial community function, particularly in the context of climate change.
📧 li.zhao@umu.se
Yu Zhao
Postdoctoral Researcher
Yu Zhao is a postdoc in Capo lab. His research focuses on agricultural activities and shifts in human–environment relationships since the Holocene.
📧 yzhao@niglas.ac.cn

PhD students

Júlia Dordal Soriano
PhD Student
Júlia Dordal Soriano is a PhD student in Andrea Garcia Bravo group at the ICM-CSIC (Barcelona). Her current research focuses on the study of microbial processes and environmental factors that control mercury transformations in the global ocean.
📧 julia.dordal.18@gmail.com
Nicola Gambardella
PhD Student
Nicola Gambardella is a PhD student from Catarina Magalhães group at "LaCaixa" INPhINIT fellowship program at CIIMAR (Porto, Portugal) working on prokaryotic communities involved in permafrost N and Hg cycling with molecular and bioinformatic approaches.
📧 ngambardella@ciimar.up.pt
Sébastien Leveque
PhD Student
Sébastien Leveque is a PhD student from Nick Kamenos group investigating the acclimation and adaptation of the coral holobiont. He mainly focuses on the symbiotic community composition within coral reefs using molecular ecology tools.
📧 sebastien.leveque@umu.se
Yangzheng Liu
PhD Student
Yangzheng Liu is a visiting PhD student in Changwei Lü group at Inner Mongolia University (China). His research focuses on lake environmental change and paleoecological reconstruction, integrating sedDNA and geochemical proxies to resolve coupled climate–human drivers at centennial–millennial scales.
Anton Sandström
PhD Student
Anton Sandström is a PhD student from Per Stenberg group exploring the ecosystems of the Skåne region using unique eDNA sets from air filters to study agricultural pathogens.
📧 anton.sandstrom01@umu.se
Meifang Zhong
PhD Student
Meifang Zhong is a PhD student in Capo lab working on microbial metabolisms of deoxygenated marine systems with molecular (paleo)ecology approaches (metagenomics, metatranscriptomics, MAGs).
📧 meifang.zhong@umu.se
