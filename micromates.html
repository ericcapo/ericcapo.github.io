🎴 MicroMates – Oceanic Realms

🌊 11 realms · 55 cards · Happy Families
🎯 How to play: Collect all 5 cards of a realm. Ask another player: “Do you have Baltic Cyanobacteria from the Baltic Sea realm?” If yes, take it and go again; if not, draw from the pile. First to collect 3 complete realms wins!
Quick rules (2–6 players): Shuffle 55 cards. Deal 7 each. Youngest starts. Ask for a card from a realm you already own. If the opponent has it, take it and continue; otherwise draw one. When you complete a realm (5 cards), show it. First with 3 realms wins!
