🎴 MicroMates - Oceanic Realms

How to play: Collect all 5 cards of a realm (e.g., Baltic Sea, California Coast etc). On your turn, ask another player for a specific card (e.g., “Do you have the yellow mate from the Baltic Sea?”). If they have it, you take it and go again. If not, draw from the pile. The first player to collect 3 complete realms wins! Card types are : 🟡 Light, 🩷 Nitrogen, 🔵 Sulfur, 🟢 Carbon, 🟣 Special

Rules (2–6 players): Shuffle all 50 cards. Deal 7 cards each. Youngest starts. On your turn, ask any player for a card from a realm you already own. If they have it, take it and continue; if not, draw 1 card from the pile. When you complete an realm (all 5 cards), show it and place it face up. First to collect 3 realm wins!
